Our Location

Nestled in the heart of the scenic Whispering Pines Valley, Sunshine School is located in the charming town of Riverview Springs. This idyllic setting, just a short drive from the bustling city of Meadowbrook, offers the perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility. Surrounded by rolling hills, lush forests, and the gentle flow of the Clearwater River, our campus provides an inspiring environment where students can learn, grow, and thrive.

Getting Here

Sunshine School is easily accessible by road and public transportation, with regular bus services connecting Riverview Springs to Meadowbrook and neighboring towns. For those traveling by car, the school is just off Highway 42, with ample parking available on campus. The closest train station, Meadowbrook Central, is a 20-minute drive away, offering convenient connections to major cities across the region.
