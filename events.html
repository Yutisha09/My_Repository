Annual Traditions

Spring Festival

Each spring, Sunshine School comes alive with our Spring Festival, a celebration of creativity, culture, and community. The festival features student performances, art exhibitions, cultural displays, and a variety of food stalls representing cuisines from around the world.

Graduation Ceremony

Our Graduation Ceremony is a proud tradition at Sunshine School, celebrating the achievements of our senior class as they prepare to embark on the next chapter of their lives. This event is a special time for students, families, and teachers to reflect on the journey and look forward to the future.

Charity Run

The Sunshine School Charity Run is an annual event that brings together students, staff, and the community to raise funds for local charities. Participants can run, walk, or jog along a designated route, with all proceeds going to support a good cause. It's a day of fitness, fun, and giving back.
