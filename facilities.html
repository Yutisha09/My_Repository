At Sunshine School, we are committed to providing our students with state-of-the-art facilities that support a well-rounded educational experience. Our campus is designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and innovation, ensuring that every student has the resources they need to succeed.

Academic Facilities

Modern Classrooms: Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology, including interactive whiteboards, high-speed internet, and digital learning tools. Designed to be flexible and adaptable, these spaces can be configured for traditional lectures, group work, or hands-on activities.

Science Laboratories: Our fully equipped science labs offer students the opportunity to explore biology, chemistry, and physics through practical experiments. Safety is a top priority, with all labs featuring modern safety equipment and protocols.

Library and Media Center: The Sunshine School library is a hub of knowledge, offering an extensive collection of books, academic journals, and digital resources. Our media center provides students with access to computers, printers, and multimedia tools for research, projects, and presentations.

Technology Center: Our cutting-edge technology center features computer labs, robotics workstations, and a coding studio, where students can learn and experiment with the latest in digital technology, programming, and robotics.

Arts and Creative Spaces

Art Studios: Sunshine School’s art studios are bright, open spaces where students can explore various forms of visual art, from painting and drawing to sculpture and digital media. The studios are equipped with professional-grade materials and tools to inspire creativity.

Music Rooms: Our dedicated music rooms are soundproofed and fully equipped with instruments for individual practice, group lessons, and ensemble rehearsals. Whether it's classical, jazz, or contemporary music, students have the opportunity to develop their musical talents.

Theater and Performance Hall: Our state-of-the-art theater is a versatile space for drama, dance, and other performances. With professional lighting, sound systems, and a spacious stage, students can showcase their talents in front of live audiences.

Sports and Recreation

Sports Complex: The Sunshine School sports complex includes a full-size gymnasium, indoor basketball and volleyball courts, and a fitness center. Our facilities are designed to support a wide range of sports and physical activities, promoting health and wellness.

Outdoor Fields: Our extensive outdoor facilities include soccer fields, a running track, tennis courts, and a swimming pool. Surrounded by natural beauty, these spaces encourage students to stay active and enjoy the outdoors.

Adventure Playground: Our adventure playground is a safe and exciting space for younger students to play, explore, and develop their motor skills. With climbing structures, slides, and a variety of play equipment, it's a favorite spot for our youngest learners.

Student Life

Cafeteria and Dining Hall: Our cafeteria offers nutritious and delicious meals, catering to a variety of dietary preferences and needs. The dining hall is a welcoming space where students can enjoy their meals and socialize with friends.

Student Lounge: The student lounge is a comfortable space where students can relax, study, or hang out with friends between classes. It features cozy seating, study pods, and recreational activities like table tennis and board games.

Gardens and Green Spaces: Sunshine School’s campus is dotted with beautiful gardens, walking paths, and quiet areas where students can unwind and connect with nature. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our green spaces, which include a student-tended vegetable garden and native plant areas.

Safety and Well-being

Health and Wellness Center: Our on-campus Health and Wellness Center is staffed by qualified medical professionals who provide routine health services, counseling, and wellness programs to support students' physical and mental well-being.

Security: Sunshine School takes the safety of our students seriously. Our campus is monitored by a comprehensive security system, including CCTV cameras, controlled access points, and a dedicated security team to ensure a safe and secure learning environment.
